GCI welfare association was established to
cater for the general welfare of the members of the church by way of assistance,
participation or contribution to the welfare of the members
especially in times in need but from a corporate perspective
The association took effect in October 2024
where members registered by way of paying a registration fee of Ksh.200.
As at end 30th April 2025,
a total of 105 members had registered in to the association.
Membership to the association is voluntary but
one must be a member of GCI Thindigua church.
